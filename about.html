About Tesla Wudineh

Our journey, values, and commitment to excellence

Our History

Tesla Wudineh Electrical & Electronics Maintenance was founded in 2008 by Wudineh Debebe, a certified electrical engineer with a passion for solving complex electrical challenges. What started as a small workshop in Baty has grown into one of Ethiopia's most trusted electrical service providers.

Over the past 15 years, we've expanded our services to cover residential, commercial, and industrial clients across the country. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind our growth.

2008
Founded in Baty with 3 employees
2012
Expanded to commercial electrical services
2015
Opened regional office in Hawassa
2018
Launched solar power division
2023
Serving clients nationwide with 50+ technicians
