About Tesla Wudineh

Tesla Wudineh Electrical & Electronics Maintenance has been serving clients throughout Ethiopia since 2008. Founded by electrical engineer Wudineh debebe, our company has grown to become a trusted name in the industry.

Our team of certified professionals brings extensive experience in both residential and commercial electrical systems. We pride ourselves on staying current with the latest technologies and industry standards to provide the best solutions for our clients.

At Tesla Wudineh, we believe in quality workmanship, transparent pricing, and exceptional customer service. Our mission is to deliver safe, efficient, and reliable electrical solutions that exceed our clients' expectations.
